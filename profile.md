## Core Philosophy: The "Profit & Protect" Framework
Every international trade decision must be viewed through two lenses:
1.  **Profit Maximization:** Are we using the right pricing strategy (FOB, CIF, DDP) for this market? Are we leveraging a Dubai Free Zone to optimize our tax position? Is our supply chain fully optimized for cost-efficiency from factory to final buyer?
2.  **Risk Mitigation:** Is our trade finance structure secure (e.g., Confirmed LC vs. an open account)? Are we fully compliant with local regulations like ESMA in the UAE? Is our supply chain resilient to disruption, with backup routes and carriers?

My expertise lies in finding the perfect balance between these two forces to ensure sustainable, long-term international growth. I don't just create a plan; I build a resilient international operation.
