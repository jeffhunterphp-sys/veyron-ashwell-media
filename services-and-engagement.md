# Services & Engagement Models: Ashwell Trade Solutions

My consulting services are designed to be flexible, providing the precise level of strategic support that your business needs to succeed globally. I offer three primary engagement models:

---

### 1. Strategic Advisory Retainer

This model is for businesses that need ongoing, high-level strategic guidance from an experienced international trade expert. I act as your fractional "Head of International Trade," providing consistent support to your leadership team.

**Best For:** Companies actively managing or expanding their international operations who need a consistent strategic sounding board.

**Includes:**
-   Monthly strategy sessions and market updates.
-   On-demand support for urgent challenges (e.g., supply chain disruptions, contract negotiations).
-   Proactive opportunity analysis for new markets and products.
-   Review of ongoing operations to identify cost-saving and efficiency improvements.

---

### 2. Project-Based Consulting

This is a focused, end-to-end engagement designed to solve a specific, high-stakes international trade challenge. We define a clear scope, timeline, and set of deliverables.

**Best For:** Companies facing a specific, mission-critical objective.

**Common Projects Include:**
*   **Go-to-Market Strategy & Execution:** A complete A-to-Z plan for entering a new country, from market research to finding your first distributor.
*   **Trade Finance Structuring:** Designing and implementing secure payment structures (Letters of Credit, SBLCs) for high-value deals.
*   **Supply Chain & Logistics Audit:** A comprehensive review of your current logistics network to identify and execute significant cost-saving opportunities.
*   **UAE Free Zone Setup:** Full-service advisory on selecting, registering, and operationalizing your business within the optimal UAE Free Zone (e.g., JAFZA, DMCC).

---

### 3. Intensive Strategy Workshop

A one- or two-day intensive workshop with your leadership team to deep-dive into a specific challenge and develop an actionable strategic plan.

**Best For:** Companies that have the internal team to execute but need expert guidance to build the right strategy.

**Workshop Topics:**
*   "De-risking Your First Export Deal"
*   "Building a Resilient Global Supply Chain"
*   "Your UAE & MENA Market Entry Blueprint"
